About Us
We work together with our high net worth clients, including families, charities, and endowments to understand or develop their general investment management strategies. We then identify or create investment opportunities that will help them reach their objectives. In our execution of these services, we combine decisive execution with a consistent and highly customized personal approach to service to our clients. When the situation demands, we can rely on a vast and deep network of people and resources to be of assistance.
Our Principles
Commitment We leverage and fully utilize the experiences and intellectual abilities of our team, as well as their network of relationships. We make and place investments in special opportunities and alternative vehicles, and can also broker and deal in security and derivative products across all major markets.
Personal Approach We care about the goals of our clients and can help define them, where needed. Each client is special in our eyes and we tailor our services according to the financial profile, lifestyle, and tolerance for risk of each of them, and then embark on a journey together to execute on our plans.
Flexibility Above all, we offer full service and informed counsel based on our desire to accommodate our clients. We are flexible and responsive to any needs, as they may change.
Our Values
As every client is important with special or unique circumstances, there are four essential attributes that stand out at the core of our value system, defining the DNA and culture of our firm. They are as follows:
Reliability
We always honor the trust of our clients. We consciously practice discretion, consistency, and transparency in all of our dealings. We take pride in being a dependable and reliable partner and do not take the level of confidence placed in us and our work, for granted. The services and manner in which we provide and execute them are the mirror image of that attitude.
Our firm stands and is built around team work. We are staffed as a collaborative organization across all our different strategy and investment groups, with each member and their input playing a valuable role in delivery in favor of our clients. Given the experience, demonstrated talent, and intellectual capital among our associates and partners, we are confident coordinating the entire investment process from strategy to execution, with consideration for the perspective at multiple levels within our ranks. We diligently work to engage all partners to build consensus and take advantage of their insight.
Collaboration
Professionalism
Our track record is the testimony of our dedicated professionals. The standard that we hold ourselves to as a firm is directly reflected in the disciplined approach of our partners and our associates. Their integrity, relentless attitude, and careful attention to every detail are the cornerstones of our business and service to clients.
We Innovate across all dimensions of our firm. We practice our business in a way that enables us to always be in a position to tie creative solutions to the special situations and opportunities that we find when needed, so that we have the best chance to deliver superior results and add additional alpha to each client’s portfolio.
Creativity
