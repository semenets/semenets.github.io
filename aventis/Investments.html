Investment Criteria
We invest in a select group, deeply vetted companies. Our ideal opportunities are companies , ventures, projects, and funds , who have developed a clear concept or product that resonates with its target market. The managers and entrepreneurs involved with these projects are uniquely positioned to tap into opportunities that the markets and trends where they invest present in the marketplace. We are interested in proof of concept that needs capital to be scaled and support plans and models that allow for sustainable models of consistent growth. We invest in companies with a minimum of $1M in revenue, and look to invest at least $750K. We do private placements as limited partner of at least $20M in funds of at least $100M in size.
Flexibility
Aventis is comfortable taking both minority and majority positions in its portfolio companies, as well is full ownership doing complete buy outs.
As a strategic partner we seek to take preferred stock in the companies where we invest, often supplemented with debt instruments.
