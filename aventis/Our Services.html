Our Services
Our firm is focused on serving a small and unique client base, that works with us on strategy and execution of capital placement, the creation of various forms of equity or debt lending and financing instruments, and the structuring of large asset pools, such as funds, trusts, and strategic direct investment vehicles.
Advisory Services
M&A We help our clients on some of their most important strategic decisions and transactions. We offer sell and buy side advice, raise capital to complete cross border M&A, complicated merger transactions and execute special committee assignments. Our select group of clients is based in 3 different continents, and include businesses, private investors, and families.
Placement We leverage the resources of our clients and rely on a deep network in finance to trade in a variety of securities and asset classes globally. We not only help to make investment decisions, but also create, hedge and structure investment opportunities ourselves to manage risks across the entire range of interests of our clients. By listening to our clients and understanding their objectives, our strong expertise and analytical skills are optimally deployed to not only provide the best advice, but also to find the best solutions, and financial terms possible.

Investment Coordination
We capture all relevant details and unique circumstances of your financial situation and will help you understand them within the context of the opportunities that we present with you. We support you in a myriad of wealth and financial related activities, including cash flow analyses, risk management, investment performance, fund placement, budgeting, and many more. We coordinate the full range of facets related to the investment process, including valuations, due diligence, establishment of risk tolerance, and determining acceptable rates for returns on investment. We are about producing superior returns for our clients. We try to achieve that in a cost efficient way and by paying attention to the smallest of detail and by making insightful contributions to thought leadership. We stay up to date on the happenings in the financial word globally, while integrating your personal goals and needs, business interests, investments, or day to day issues, into the investment decisions we make. Where needed we can collaborate with your advisors and professional specialists, such as bankers, lawyers and accountants, to ensure effective implementations of your plans and strategies. We are focused on finding solutions and successful execution is our highest priority.

Culture & Legacy
Our programs, opportunities, and instruments are tailored for ultra high net worth families or individuals, corporations and funds. We engage all clients in the necessary meaningful discussion to guide their financial moves with Aventis.

As a firm we understand that dissipation of wealth is of the utmost priority and concern among our client base.

We are committed to you as our client to present you a firm culture that fosters an open exchange of responsibilities and opportunities where the performance of high stakes transactions and for successful wealth dealings can thrive.
Symbiotic Relationships Aventis is a firm that helps and supports your ambitions and define your legacy through enhancing your network and access to special opportunities. Our partners and associates have all developed their own valuable professional networks and relationships that can be integrated with the technical aspects of our investment process, or added to the preparation and communication, as functions of the personal relationship we are building where even more unconventional opportunities that fit with the lifestyle or legacy objectives that you may have, can be explored and have lasting outcomes.
Preparation By nature of our services, we will get to know each other. Aventis’ focus on building a symbiotic connection with clients means that we will work hard and diligently to grasp your understandings about financial and wealth concepts to develop a solid and shared foundation for informed decision making.
Skills development Our managers and associates will engage and invite you to events and opportunities to expand and build on your interests and value systems when and where that is appropriate.
Mentoring and education We engage clients and their associates with well trained professionals to introduce insightful perspectives on topics and subjects of interest, based on personal passions and interests.
Authentic content Aventis organizes, sponsors, and produces its own programs and events that communicate and signal to the outside world what the firm stands for. They are intended to inspire, entertain, or inform and will give our client an additional opportunity to get to know us.
Communication Together we build a platform for effective communication, that will instill a level of confidence that your interests and ambitions have an opportunity to flourish and become a reality.
