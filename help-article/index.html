Creating a product Last updated: Feb 09, 2017 From the Products screen, you can create new individual products in Veeqo directly. These will not be visible on any of your sales channels to start with. There are two types of products that can be created:
  • Add a simple product.
  • Add variations to a product, such as sizes and colours.
Creating a simple product A simple product is a product without any variations. 1. From the Products page, select the New Product button. 2. Add your product details:
  • Product name * - Pick a name that best describes the product.
  • SKU * - Add a unique character string to identify the product
  • Retail price excluding tax * - The price the product is sold for without the tax.
  • Stock quantity for each stock location - This is the total amount of stock you have for this product in that particular warehouse.
  • Brand name - The make of the product you’re creating.
  • Barcode (EAN, UPC) - The unique code associated with each individual product.
  • Origin country - The country in which the product has been manufactured.
  • Description - A summary about the product’s benefits and features.
Note: Only fields with a ‘*’ are the required fields you need to create your product, if you want to create the product quickly. Additional fields will be useful, if you choose to list your products on an eBay or Amazon store later.
3. Adding dimensions and weight to the product are recommended so your product will be ready to ship. Select either ‘cm’ or ‘inches’ and then add the width, height and depth of the product. When adding the weight, it will be set to the default unit for your company (Grams or ounces). 4. The pricing and stock options are recommended:
  • Cost price - The average cost price paid for the product (without sales tax).
  • Tax rate % - The percentage of sales tax to be added on top of the retail price when the product is sold.
  • Retail price including tax - The price of the product is sold for with the tax added on.
  • Minimum stock level - When the product reaches this level, stock will be reordered.
  • Reorder quantity - The quantity to be reordered once the product has reached it’s minimum stock level.
  • Sublocation - per stock location - The exact stock location for the product in each of your stock locations.
5. This is a simple product, so don’t worry about the add options section. Head straight to adding images. Select the add image button to add up to 9 images from your computer to the product. Once your images have uploaded, you can drag and drop them into the order you’d like them to be shown on your product. The image in position 1 will be the default image used for the product. Use the magnifying glass icon to view a larger preview of an image and the trash can icon to delete an image. 6. Once you’re ready to save your product, select the create product button in the bottom right and you’ll see the product appear in the main products list. Creating a product with variations A product that has variations, comes in other options such as sizes and colours. 1. Complete the details, dimensions and weight, and pricing and stock sections from creating a simple product above. 2. Use the add options section to create your variants. Select the add more variants button to create a variant and add it’s individual details. Use the add more variants button to add another variant. If you’ve made a mistake and want to remove a variant, use the trash can icon. 3. In the images section of a variant product, images can be assigned to individual variants once uploaded. Select an image and use the assign to all button to add the image to all variants. To add an image to certain variants, select the image and then the particular variants and use the assign to selected button. 4. Once you’re ready to save your product, select the create product button in the bottom right and you’ll see the product with it’s variants appear in the main products list.
